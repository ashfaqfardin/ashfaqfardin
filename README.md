## About

Computer scientist and AI researcher with a background in software engineering, data science, and community leadership. Currently reading for an MSc in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Surrey. Previously led the IEEE Computer Society student chapter at Independent University, Bangladesh, where I organised technical events and represented the university in IEEEXtreme.

I work at the intersection of machine learning, software development, and human-centred technology — and I care as much about building things well as building the right things.
