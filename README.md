## About

I am a researcher and engineer currently pursuing an MSc in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Surrey. My work focuses on building AI systems that are useful, interpretable, and human-centred, with a particular focus on educational applications.

I spent over a year as a research intern at IUB's Center for Computational and Data Sciences, where I published at CHI 2025, contributed to the SomajGyaan dataset published at IJCNLP-AACL 2025, and began exploring Visual Document Understanding. I also attended the ACM SIGCHI Winter School in Sri Lanka, mentored by researchers from Georgia Tech, Stanford, and Google.
